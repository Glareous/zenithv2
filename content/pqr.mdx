### üìù Input Fields (Web PQR Form)

<br />

These fields form the structure of the PQR entry:

#### First Name
The customer's given name. Used to personalize communication and support identification.

#### Last Name
Customer's family name. Used with first name for full identification.

#### Phone
Primary contact number. Useful for urgent follow-up in high-risk cases.

#### Email
Customer's email address. Used for notifications and formal responses.

#### City
Location of the customer. Helps identify regional risk patterns or regulatory considerations.

#### Document Type
Type of identification (CC, CE, Passport). Determines how the entity validates user identity.

#### Document Number
The ID number associated with the selected document type. Helps uniquely identify the customer in the organization's systems.

#### Status
Represents the lifecycle state of the PQR. Default is *Processing* until resolved.

#### Message
The **core text** entered by the customer.  
This is the primary input for the AI, and includes the user's request, complaint, or claim.

<br />

<Callout type="success">
The <strong>Message</strong> field drives 80% of the AI logic: topic classification, emotion detection, urgency evaluation, and risk scoring depend on its content.
</Callout>
